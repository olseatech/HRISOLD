## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users: barangay and government HR staff, managers, and authorized employees using the system for daily workforce operations.
- Usage context: desktop-first office workflows with regular tablet/mobile access for approvals and quick status checks.
- Core jobs: manage employee records, monitor attendance, process leave, maintain payroll and settings with high confidence and low friction.

### Brand Personality
- Official
- Trustworthy
- Efficient
- Tone: calm, clear, and accountable; avoids startup-like novelty and decorative excess.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Direction: Government Minimal White interface.
- Theme: white-first surfaces with navy structure and blue-plus-teal accent system.
- Visual posture: compact but readable data presentation, strong hierarchy, restrained effects, and predictable interactions.
- Branding: include official government seal/logo placement in login and sidebar identity blocks.

### Design Principles
1. Prioritize operational clarity over decoration: every visual element must support task completion.
2. Keep density balanced: compact spacing for more visible context without sacrificing readability.
3. Use color semantically: blue for primary actions/navigation, teal as secondary accent, and consistent state colors.
4. Preserve reliability cues: stable layout patterns, visible focus states, and accessible contrast (WCAG 2.1 AA).
5. Evolve in place: no backend behavior changes; redesign is implemented safely through view/CSS/JS refinements only.
